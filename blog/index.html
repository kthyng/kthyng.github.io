SciPy Conference 2018

Jul 13, 2018

The Scientific Python conference (SciPy) was another success! I co-chaired the Earth, Ocean, and Geoscience mini-symposium with Scott Collis and we had a bunch of great abstracts submitted. This lead to a series of nice talks and posters.

I spoke at the conference this year about the website I've been working on for the Texas General Land Office (TGLO) which combines together operational data and model output along with all available past information.



Website for Interacting w/ Oceanographic Data & Numerical Model Output | SciPy 2018 | Kristen Thyng

The Texas Automated Buoy System (TABS) has been recording and serving online velocity and other in situ water data since 1995. We have recently modernized the website to include more data and operational numerical model output, and to improve ease of use.
