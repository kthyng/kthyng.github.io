2014 HAB Prediction

Jun 25, 2015

Last fall I made a prediction that there would not be a major harmful algal bloom in Texas caused by the phytoplankton Karenia brevis. I would like to follow that up by saying that we were right!



Texas Parks and Wildlife issues updates about the state of harmful algal blooms. The website shows that while there were some low levels of cell abundance found in August and September, there was never a major bloom.
